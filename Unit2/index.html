Which two sites are you most inspired by:

The two websites I am inspired by are FourSeasons.com and Homes.com

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size or balance?

Four Seasons

I believe Four Seasons exhibits effective use of space because they strategically managed to get tons of information on the homepage without it feeling overwhelming. The main image changes every few seconds, but the reservation information (which overlaps the main image) stays - which is ultimatley the end goal here. They are luring you in with the oversized beautiful images of their resorts, and making it easy to reserve with just a few clicks. I think everything is nicely balanced and clearly defined within their site.

Homes.com

What makes them similar and different?

Similar - I think they both use color effectively. Neither wesbite is boring, all wording is legible. There is hierarchy in terms of what is important and I think the purpose of the website is defined from the start. Also, both websites use the center of the page to grab your attention.
Different - Homes.com has a stable background photo and Four Seasons has an everchanging background photo - of there many different resorts.
